# Anilsha's Portfolio

## About

Hello, my name is Anilsha Taveras. I am a data analyst with experience in cleaning and parsing datasets to extract meaningful insights. I am completing my Master of Science in Data Analytics Engineering at Northeastern University, and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from California State University, East Bay. 

When preparing data for analysis, the cleansing and accuracy of the data is most important. I have developed my skills to identify patterns in data by laying out the most fundamental steps in analytics — identifying business requirements, the collection and cleansing of data for accuracy and completeness, analysis using statistical techniques, and visualization of the findings to suggest business decisions. In fact, 90% of an analyst's position is to ensure their data is clear, concise, and accurate before proceeding to analysis.

I continue to learn new techniques that assist in the collection and analysis of data, enriching my education through practice with Python, SQL, ETL processes using softwares such as Talend, etc. This portfolio showcases some of the projects I have worked on relating to Data Analysis and Data Engineering.
